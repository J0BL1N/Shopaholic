Refund & Return Policy

Thank you for supporting Shopaholic Approved. Please read our guidelines regarding returns and refunds below.

1. Return window

We accept return inquiries within 14 days of delivery receipt. To start a request, please email us with your order name, item details, and photo proof of the unopened package.

2. Unopened Condition Requirement

All returned products must be in their original, unopened, and unused state.
- Blind Boxes & Mystery Items: If the packaging box flap, foil wrap, or internal plastic bag seal has been cut, ripped, or tampered with, the item is considered final sale and is ineligible for returns or refunds.

3. Outer Box & Retail Package Condition

Outer packaging cartons and figure retail boxes serve to protect the collectible figure inside. While we package items meticulously, minor retail box creases, minor corner dents, or scuffs can occur during international supplier transport or postal sorting. We do not offer returns, discounts, or replacements for minor box packaging imperfections if the figure inside is undamaged.

4. Defective or Damaged Figures

If your figure is broken or has a severe manufacturer paint defect, please email us within 5 days of delivery. Please include clear photos of the issue. We'll work with the distributor to secure a replacement or issue store credit.
