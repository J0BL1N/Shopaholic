Shipping Policy

Welcome to the Shopaholic Approved Shipping Policy. As collectors ourselves, we recognize how critical shipping care is. We treat every order with the utmost precision.

1. Handling & Processing Speed

- In-Stock Items: We process and package in-stock items within 1–3 business days.
- Preorder Items: Orders containing both in-stock and preorder items will be held and shipped together once the preorder items arrive. If you want in-stock items immediately, please submit separate requests.

2. Packaging Protection

We wrap all blind boxes, plushies, and figures securely in bubble wrap. Everything is shipped in sturdy, corrugated cardboard boxes surrounded by bubble pillows or kraft paper packing sheets to absorb shocks during transit. We never ship fragile figures in soft mailers.

3. Carrier Transit times & Delays

Standard shipping transit takes 3–7 business days. Tracking info is emailed automatically. While we hand off packages quickly, carrier delays due to peak holiday volumes, sorting errors, or weather are out of our control.

4. Preorder Schedules

Estimated arrival times (ETAs) are calculated using manufacturer predictions. These are estimates and can shift due to international supply chain bottlenecks, port delays, or customs inspection hold-ups. We commit to email updates if there are significant schedule adjustments.
