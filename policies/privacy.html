Privacy Policy

At Shopaholic Approved, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data for our independent collectible boutique.

1. Local Storage Usage

We utilize your browser's local storage (under the key `sa_cart`) to preserve the items in your shopping cart. This is stored entirely on your local machine and enables you to browse and save items across sessions.

2. Sourcing & Contact Request Form Data

Information you input into our item request or contact forms (including name, email, character details, and budgets) is handled directly to coordinate orders. In this early-stage website demo, data is handled client-side and is not sent to external servers.

3. Brand Trademarks & Cookies

We do not track you across other sites, nor do we sell user data to advertising networks. References to third-party brand names or character items are exclusively used to identify the products being sold.
