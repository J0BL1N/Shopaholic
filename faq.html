Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preorders, shipping speeds, box protection, and returns.

Preorders allow you to reserve high-demand collectibles before they are released or arrive in stock. Please note that Estimated Times of Arrival (ETAs) are not guaranteed. Supplier shipments can be delayed by production queues, ocean freight shipping times, and customs clearances. No payment is processed during checkout on this demo; ordering and fulfillment arrangements will be coordinated with you individually via email once stock arrives.
For in-stock items, we process orders within 1–3 business days. Once shipped, standard transit takes 3–7 business days depending on location. Please note that unexpected shipping delays can occasionally happen due to weather, holiday volumes, or carrier logistics, which are out of our immediate control. We'll always provide tracking so you can monitor your package.
As avid collectors ourselves, we know how important box condition is! We wrap all items carefully in bubble wrap and use sturdy cardboard shipping boxes with paper packing material to prevent items from shifting during transit. We want your collectibles to arrive at your door in the same condition they left our shelves.
We separate packaging boxes (like blind box outer cartons or retail figure boxes) from the actual collectible toy inside. While we make every effort to pack boxes securely, minor cosmetic creases, corner scuffs, or dents on the packaging box can occur in transit from suppliers or delivery companies. We do not offer refunds or replacements for packaging boxes that suffer minor wear as long as the collectible figure inside is completely intact and undamaged.
You can request a return on unopened, unused items in their original packaging within 14 days of receipt. For blind boxes and mystery figures, the item's outer foil seal/box flap must be completely unopened and untampered with. Once a blind box is opened, it is considered a final sale and cannot be returned or refunded unless the figure inside is proven to have an original manufacturer defect.
