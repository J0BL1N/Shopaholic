About Shopaholic Approved

Welcome! We are Shopaholic Approved, a cute collector boutique curated by Fabi and Jay. We specialize in bringing rare, hand-picked collectibles and trendy plushies directly to fellow enthusiast communities.

The Story of Fabi & Jay

What started as a personal obsession with blind boxes, plush keychains, and aesthetic room decor quickly grew into a full-scale sourcing adventure. We found ourselves spent hours browsing international distributor sheets, hunting down limited-run restocks, and trying to secure hard-to-find variations. We realized other collectors share the exact same hunt! Thus, Shopaholic Approved was born to make cute sourcing accessible and transparent.

Meet Our Mascot (The Green Scarf)

If you look closely at our boutique notes and symbols, you'll spot our logo mascot wearing a signature green scarf. He represents the curious collector—always seeking rare discoveries, looking under every leaf, and making sure every single find is verified, authentic, and Shopaholic Approved!

Our Curation Promise

Every product you see in our shop is sourced from authorized distributors or curated from reputable designers. We pack every box by hand with custom bubble wrap and structural support, because we believe receiving your item should feel like opening a gift to yourself.
